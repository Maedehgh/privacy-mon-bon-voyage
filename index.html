Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 8, 2026

Mon Bon Voyage values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Mon Bon Voyage mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Location data (optional): If you grant permission, location access may be used to show your position on the map and help start routes from your current location.
  • App usage data: Basic, anonymous usage information may be used to improve app performance and user experience.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to provide core app features such as trip planning, map functionality, and improving overall usability.

Third-Party Services

Mon Bon Voyage uses third-party services such as Google Maps to display maps and locations. Payments and subscriptions are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. These services may collect information as governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Trip data and preferences are stored locally on your device or using Apple-provided services. We do not operate our own external servers for storing personal user data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Subscriptions and Payments

Premium subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple. Mon Bon Voyage does not collect or have access to your payment information.

Children’s Privacy

Mon Bon Voyage does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that such information has been collected, please contact us so we can remove it.

Your Choices

You can manage or revoke app permissions, including location access, through your device settings. Subscriptions can be managed through your Apple ID.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
