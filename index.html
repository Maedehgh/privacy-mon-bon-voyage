Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 8, 2026

Mon Bon Voyage values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Mon Bon Voyage mobile application.

Information We Collect

Mon Bon Voyage may collect the following information:

  • Location data (optional): If you grant permission, location access may be used to show your position on the map and help start routes from your current location.
  • App usage data: Basic, anonymous usage information may be used to improve app performance and user experience.

How We Use Information

Information is used only to provide core app features, such as trip planning, map functionality, and improving overall usability.

Data Storage

Trip data and preferences are stored locally on your device or using Apple-provided services. We do not operate our own external data servers for personal user data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Subscriptions and Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. Mon Bon Voyage does not collect, store, or have access to your payment information.

Your Choices

You can manage or revoke app permissions, including location access, at any time through your device settings. You may also manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID account settings.
